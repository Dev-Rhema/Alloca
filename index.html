Manifesto

Traditional launchpad structures are slowly fading out. Fixed prices, shallow liquidity, and unsafe custody have left investors vulnerable while projects struggle to gain traction. Communities deserve better than this. That's why Alloca has decided to pivot, moving away from the traditional launchpad structure and into bonding curves that enable fair, liquid, community-driven fundraising.

Alloca V2 reimagines fundraising. We're creating Internet Capital Markets on Monad, where serious projects and memes can coexist. Permissionless memecoins, ICM sales, and Chad Sales with custom bonding curves, will all be available within the same protocol. Driven by bonding curves and migration triggers, we will provide true price discovery without any compromise.

Alloca isn't just a launchpad. It's an entire ecosystem. At the core we have the Molandaks and Purple Frens NFT collections deeply integrated into the Alloca flywheel. With 100% of net profits cycling back into our ecosystem through buybacks and rewards, every step forward strengthens both our token and NFTs.

Internet Capital Markets on Monad starts here and we've only just begun.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Alloca?

+

Alloca is the first launchpad on the Monad blockchain. It provides a robust platform for project creators to launch presales and fair launches, and for investors to discover high-potential opportunities in the DeFi and NFT space on Monad.

How do I participate in the public round of $ALLO?

+

The $ALLO public sale is whitelist (WL) only. All OG roles will automatically get a WL spot for the public sale. Additional WL spots will be distributed through various campaigns and giveaways closer to the launch.

What are Chad Sales?

+

Chad Sales are exclusive presales vetted and incubated by the Alloca team. Only holders of top three tiers (Griffin, Phoenix, Leviathan) can participate. Allocation is distributed as follows: Griffin (10%), Phoenix (30%), Leviathan (60%).

What is the Purple Frens NFT collection?

+

The Purple Frens NFT collection consists of 1,111 generative PFPs featuring unique purple frogs. Holders receive numerous benefits, including:

  • 20% revenue share from Alloca
  • Staking rewards in $ALLO
  • Access to Unicorn tier for Chad Sales
  • 1of1 holders receive Leviathan tier
  • Private chat for holders
  • A mystery reward (details yet to be revealed)

What is the Fren Pass?

+

The Fren Pass is the only way to secure a Purple Fren before launch. With only 100 available, it is awarded to outstanding community members and through exclusive giveaways and raffles. Winners will also be featured with a unique badge on Twitter.

What is the Molandaks collection?

+

The Molandaks NFT collection is a community-focused, free-mint collection of 5,555 unique Molandak-inspired NFTs, created with the blessing of the original Molandak creator. Get WL through games, giveaways, role upgrades, and from being active in the server.
